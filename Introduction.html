Welcome to GEARSTORE

With over 10 years of experience in the computer components market, we are proud to be a reputable supplier for individuals and businesses in Vietnam. It features a user-friendly interface, a secure payment gateway, and a comprehensive inventory of computer components and repair services. We have designed, developed, and implemented the website to provide a convenient and reliable platform for customers to purchase computer components and repair services. The project has been a valuable learning experience for us, and we are proud of what we have accomplished.
